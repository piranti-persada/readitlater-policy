# Account Deletion
Users can delete their account directly within the app.

## Account Deletion Procedure
1. Open the Pile app  
2. Navigate to "Settings"  
3. Tap "Account Settings"  
4. Tap "Delete account"  
5. Confirm deletion  
6. Verify your identity by entering your password or signing in again with Google (if applicable)  
7. Re-enter your username to confirm.

## Alternative Method
If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting:
**info@piranti-persada.id**

## Data Deletion
All user data associated with your account will be permanently deleted within 14 days of the request. This includes all personal information and account-related data.
